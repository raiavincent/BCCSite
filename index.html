About Us

Founded in the fall of 2021, Bushwick Chess Club has formed a community of players, new and experienced alike. The community has flourished with consistent club nights and tournaments, with an engaged community.
The club nights are consistently full, but that should not discourage new visitors. Rapid (10+0) and blitz (5+0) tournaments are held monthly as well, with cash prizes.
Bushwick Chess Club is thankful to Nook for allowing us to use their space to grow our community and chess abilities. Beer, wine, coffee, etc. is available for purchase all night, please show your support to them.
